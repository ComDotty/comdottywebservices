Who We Are

Our History

Founded in 2008 our strategy here at ComDotty Web Services is to fill the need for affordable software services for small and medium sized businesses.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple. Let us take care of your website so you can take care of your business.

Any small businessman knows that time is their most precious commodity. Time to deal with customers and suppliers, time to devote to orders, handling stock and sales, the marketing, deliveries, the staff, the accounts and a whole host of other jobs.

Here at ComDotty Web Services we know how to make the best use of the latest tools and technologies to give you the solutions you need, so you can spend time on building your business instead of managing your website.

From custom software development and databases to E-commerce as Shopify Partners. Cloud-services and analytics to cross-platform apps and server maintenance, we can help.

What's New

Latest Update

July/August 2024

Big changes of coming here at ComDotty HQ!

We've successfully gained new skills to add to our portfolio. From GDPR, Cookie Consent and Google Analytics V4 (GA-4) to AI services, marketing and new cross-platform development tools. We have become part of the Google Developer initiative, embraced .NET MAUI and Atlas database hosting and are being very active on Upwork, fiverr and Guru.

Although we have always done our best to remain flexible in the software development industry, especially when it comes to web development, there is no denying it is a fast moving environment and if you don't pay attention you can quickly get left behind. This was where we found ourselves not so long ago!

Over a period of only a few months complacency set in, and our enthusiasm for fresh projects had waned to the point where we were happy to rest on our laurels!

Thankfully fresh thinking and exposure to new requirements snapped the team out of its rut. We are invigorated, and actively developing some exciting ideas for the future!

New projects are in the pipeline, from backend web services and cross-platform apps to e-publishing and 3D printing, there are so many opportunities we can take advantage of, all that is needed is enthusiasm and a positive outlook for the future.
